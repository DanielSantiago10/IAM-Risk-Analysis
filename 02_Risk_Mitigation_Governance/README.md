### 1. Mandatory Security Awareness Program

To address undefined training frequencies, I developed a structured roadmap to transform employees into a "Human Firewall":

* **Onboarding Integration:** Mandatory security training for all new hires within their first 60 days.


* **Deployment Timeline:** A phased rollout targeting 50% staff completion within 30 days and 100% within 60 days.


* **Continuous Education:** Implementation of annual refreshers to maintain a security-first culture.



### 2. 10-Point Security Enforcement Framework

I established a high-priority checklist to reduce the organization's "Trust Surface" and ensure data integrity:

* **Technical Hardening:** Deploying updated antivirus, flagging external emails, and addressing Zero-day exploits.


* **Data Governance:** Information classification, handling, and labeling.


* **Physical & Behavioral Controls:** Enforcement of "Clean Desk" policies, workstation session-locking, and tailgating prevention.
